Our Story

Welcome to OG Restaurant, a classic family restaurant where we serve delicious and Authentic Nepali food. Our journey began in 2018 when I, along with my close friend, decided to create a place where people could enjoy heartwarming meals in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere. Our passion for food and dedication to quality has driven us from the beginning, and it continues to inspire us every day. At OG Restaurant, we believe in the joy of sharing great food with family and friends, and we strive to make every meal a memorable experience for our guests.
